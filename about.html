Meet the Artist

Sophia Laurent,
Lead Photographer

Born in Paris and trained at the École des Arts Appliqués, Sophia brought her European sensibility to New York in 2013 — and Lumière Studio was born. Her vision was simple: create photographs so beautiful that they become part of a family's identity for generations.

Today, Sophia and her carefully assembled team of specialist photographers have served over 2,400 clients — from growing families to Fortune 500 executives to first-time parents nervously holding their brand-new baby.

Her work has appeared in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and The New York Times Style Magazine, and she has been recognized with 47 industry awards for excellence in portrait photography.

Our Philosophy

Three Pillars of
Our Craft

✦

Artistic Vision

We approach every session as an editorial collaboration. Your session is curated — from styling to lighting to the final edit — with the same care as a magazine cover shoot.

◎

Technical Mastery

We invest in the finest equipment — medium-format cameras, professional lighting systems, and calibrated monitors — so every pixel of your image is perfection.

❋

Genuine Connection

The best photographs happen when subjects feel completely at ease. We prioritize relationship-building above all — because relaxed, joyful subjects create unforgettable images.
