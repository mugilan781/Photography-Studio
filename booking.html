Good to Know

Booking
Information

Everything you need to know before booking your session with AURELLE Studio.

Session Duration

60–120 Minutes

Most portrait sessions run 60–90 minutes. Family and newborn sessions are extended to 2 hours to ensure everyone is comfortable and relaxed.

Deposit Policy

50% to Confirm

A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due at the time of your session. We accept all major cards.

Rescheduling

Free with 48hr Notice

Life happens — we understand. Reschedule your session at no charge with at least 48 hours notice. We're always happy to find an alternative date.

Cancellation Policy

72-Hour Notice Required

Cancellations made 72+ hours before your session receive a full refund of the deposit. Late cancellations are non-refundable but may be applied to a future session.

Have questions? Our team is happy to help before you book.
