Privacy
Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Summary: We collect only what we need, never sell your data, and are committed to protecting your privacy. Read on for the full details.

1. Introduction

Lumière Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website lumierestudio.com and the associated photography booking services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or book our services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Fill out a booking or contact form (name, email, phone number)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter (email address)
  • Make a payment for services (handled by our payment processor)
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or in-person

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL, and device information via standard server logs and cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and confirm photography sessions
  • Process payments and send receipts
  • Deliver your final photographs via secure online gallery
  • Send session reminders and preparation guides
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send marketing communications (newsletter) with your consent
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored in your browser. We use:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function (theme preference, session state)
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how visitors use our site (via aggregated, anonymous data)
  • Marketing cookies — Used to show relevant advertisements on third-party platforms (only with consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business — such as payment processors, email delivery services, and cloud storage — under strict confidentiality agreements.

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Lumière Studio, our clients, or others.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Client photographs are stored for a minimum of 12 months after delivery. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate information
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lumierestudio.com.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
