FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before your session. Can't find your answer? Reach out to us.

Ask a Question
We recommend booking 4–6 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (spring and fall). For newborn sessions, we recommend booking during your second trimester to secure a slot — newborns are photographed within the first 10–14 days of life.
We send every client a detailed styling guide after booking. Generally, we recommend solid, neutral tones — ivory, beige, blush, navy, and charcoal work beautifully. Avoid busy patterns and neon colors. For family sessions, coordinate rather than match. Our Signature and Heirloom clients also receive a complimentary styling consultation.
Standard turnaround is 2–3 weeks from your session date. Your gallery will be delivered via a private online link. Heirloom clients and those who add the Rush Delivery option receive images within 48 hours.
Absolutely. We offer a curated selection of fine art products — including museum-quality canvas wraps, acrylic prints, heirloom albums, and framed prints. A product viewing session can be scheduled after your gallery is delivered.
We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking. Cancellations made more than 7 days before the session receive a full credit toward rescheduling. Cancellations within 7 days may forfeit the deposit. We're always happy to reschedule due to illness or emergencies — just let us know as soon as possible.
We host seasonal mini-session events 2–3 times per year, which are announced via our newsletter. Subscribe below to be the first to know. We also offer referral discounts — refer a friend and both of you receive a complimentary upgrade.
